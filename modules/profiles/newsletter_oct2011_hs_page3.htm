  IMPORTANT REMINDERS:
   
 
1. Dear parents, please ensure that the school has up-to-date telephone numbers and email addresses on record for you.
 
2. Any (confidential) student’s medical or dietary information should be forwarded to the School Nurse and Admissions Office.
 
3. Students are reminded to resume with their hair clippers.
 
4. All blazers should be dry cleaned and tears sewn together with school logo in clear view.
 
5. Students with worn blazers should endeavour to get new ones.
 
6. Weekly boarders should leave the hostel between 4 – 6 pm on Fridays and return between 4 – 5:30 pm on Sundays.
 
7. Students should come back to school with their mosquito nets and are not allowed mobile telephones in School. The Boarding Parents and Reception have mobile lines for emergency use only.
 
OCTOBER 2011 NEWSLETTER
 
Association of International Schools in Africa (AISA)
Three members of staff have attended the Association of International Schools in Africa (AISA) conference in Kenya and will be sharing their experience with the rest of the school community in due course. Their participation will greatly benefit students and teachers, which will improve quality delivery of the curriculum.
Introduction of e-Portal Link

Lekki British School launches an on-line progress-monitoring solution for parents and guardians to monitor the performance and growth of their children straight from their comfort zones, anytime anywhere.

This IT initiative includes:

  • E-portal
  • E-library (access to world wide library resource center)
  • Global partnership with Microsoft, which includes:
    1. Implementation of microsoftlive@edu
    2. MS developer platform free offering for students
    3. Global interconnectivity
Swimming becomes one of the Most Popular Activities
Hurray! The swimming pool is now in use after undergoing extensive renovations. Students have started making best use of the facility. Access to the pool is restricted with two members of staff (male and female) present at all times in addition to a qualified lifeguard.
