INTRODUCING THE NEW ACTING PRINCIPAL

I would like to seize this opportunity to introduce myself as the newly appointed Acting Principal here at Lekki British School with effect from September, 2011.  My name is Mark Richard Brown, born in South Shields, United Kingdom in 1961 and educated in Newcastle Grammar School.  Formerly served in the Royal Air Force Police followed by a First  Degree in Psychology, a Masters in Educational Psychology and a Postgraduate Certificate in Education (QTS).  I am currently in the fourth Year of a PhD

researching the relationship between poverty and educational performance in West Africa.  Since 2000 I have lived and worked in Egypt, Qatar and Sudan and have extensive Senior Management experience, including being a Principal in a reputable school in Ghana.
