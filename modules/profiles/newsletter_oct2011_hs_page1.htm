  IMPORTANT REMINDERS:
   
 
1. Dear parents, please ensure that the school has up-to-date telephone numbers and email addresses on record for you.
 
2. Any (confidential) student’s medical or dietary information should be forwarded to the School Nurse and Admissions Office.
 
3. Students are reminded to resume with their hair clippers.
 
4. All blazers should be dry cleaned and tears sewn together with school logo in clear view.
 
5. Students with worn blazers should endeavour to get new ones.
 
6. Weekly boarders should leave the hostel between 4 – 6 pm on Fridays and return between 4 – 5:30 pm on Sundays.
 
7. Students should come back to school with their mosquito nets and are not allowed mobile telephones in School. The Boarding Parents and Reception have mobile lines for emergency use only.

INTRODUCING THE NEW ACTING PRINCIPAL

I would like to seize this opportunity to introduce myself as the newly appointed Acting Principal here at Lekki British School with effect from September, 2011. My name is Mark Richard Brown, born in South Shields, United Kingdom in 1961 and educated in Newcastle Grammar School. Formerly served in the Royal Air Force Police followed by a First Degree in Psychology, a Masters in Educational Psychology and a Postgraduate Certificate in Education (QTS). I am currently in the fourth Year of a PhD researching the relationship between poverty and educational performance in West Africa. 		Since 2000 I have lived and worked in Egypt, Qatar and Sudan and have extensive Senior Management experience, including being a Principal in a reputable school in Ghana.
