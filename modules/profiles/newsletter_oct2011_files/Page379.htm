What is Opal Eye?

 

Opal Eye is a new revolutionary way to label clothing, bags and shoes that is quick and simple.  It is successfully used in schools to label uniforms and other related items in seconds. It has the following merits;

 

· No time consuming sewing

· No ironing on adhesive labels

· No unsightly names written in marker pen that washes off in the laundry process!

            

It is a small button that is etched into the garment’s owners name and is then attached to either the inside label, inside seam or on the garment using a simple hand held applicator. To remove the button, simply use the Applicator removal tool, remove the button and re-apply it to the new item of clothing. 

             Opal Eye has been developed using material that will withstand industrial laundry temperatures and is suitable for use in the domestic and industrial washing processes as well as dry cleaning. 

- It has been ergonomically designed so that it is comfortable and barely noticeable to the wearer.  Its design and size replicates a small spare button. It saves you time and provides a quick alternative to sew in and iron on labels and it won’t come off!
